Matching Cards

A matching card game, also known as Concentration or Memory, is a popular card game that requires players to find and pair identical cards. Here are the key characteristics: Gameplay: A standard deck of cards is used, with all cards facing down. Players take turns flipping over two cards at a time to reveal their faces. If the cards match, they are removed from the game. If they don’t match, the cards are turned back over, and the next player takes a turn. Objective: The goal is to clear the board by finding and pairing all the matching cards.
